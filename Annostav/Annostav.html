Our Work Showcase:

Annotsav 2026

Client: Rotary Club of
Belagavi

Project Overview

Social Santa successfully delivered end-to-end print media and event branding solutions for Annotsav 2026, one of Belagavi’s largest food and cultural festivals. From large-format structures to detailed print creatives, our team handled the entire visual identity of the event with a strong focus on impact, consistency and audience engagement.

We designed and executed a wide range of creative assets including:

Creative Approach

Our creative approach focused on theme-driven designs reflecting food, culture and celebration. We used bold and festive color palettes for high public visibility, clean typography suitable for large crowds and open spaces, and seamless integration of sponsor branding. All designs were optimized for both close-up interaction and long-distance viewing.

Execution and quality

Social Santa ensured print-ready accuracy, color consistency and premium finishing across all deliverables. Special attention was given to large-scale elements such as the entrance gate, stage and photobooth, making them key visual highlights of the event.

Project Impact

Strong first impression through impactful entrance branding High audience engagement at the photobooth Professional stage presence for performances Consistent branding across the entire venue Positive feedback from organizers and participants

Why Social Santa

Complete event branding expertise Creative and strategic design thinking End-to-end print media execution Reliable and on-time delivery Proven experience in large-scale events

Conclusion

The Annotsav 2026 event branding and print media campaign highlights Social Santa’s ability to manage large-format event visuals with precision and creativity. We transform events into memorable brand experiences through impactful design and flawless execution.
