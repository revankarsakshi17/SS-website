www.oceanplate.com

Ocean Plate represents a restaurant brand specializing in seafood and coastal cuisine. The visual identity emphasizes freshness, nature, and cultural authenticity.

Logo Design

The Ocean Plate logo reflects coastal identity through meaningful elements—green leaf for freshness, blue fish and waves for seafood authenticity, and golden tones representing sunrise and warmth. The logo must remain clean, proportional and unaltered for consistent brand recognition.

Typography

Aspekta 550 is used for English to maintain a modern, premium feel, while Sarala is used for Marathi to preserve regional authenticity. Together, they create a clean bilingual identity with strong readability across mediums.

Color Palette

The brand uses ocean blues, fresh greens and warm sunset shades inspired by coastal landscapes. Primary colors define the brand identity, while value scales create design flexibility for backgrounds, highlights, buttons, and social creatives—ensuring modern and consistent visuals.
